1st - 7th May: Cri du Chat Awareness Week

The International Cri du Chat awareness week is coming, and it's time to tell the world about Cri du Chat and what it means. Host a coffee morning, raise some money, post on your social media feeds, call your local radio station, anything!

Get Involved!

Here in the UK, the awareness week culminates in our 2016 Family Weekend in Coventry, a chance for families to get together, learn from each other, and celebrate our childrens' successes! Visit the family weekend page for more information.
