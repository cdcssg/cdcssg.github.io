The 2016 Cri du Chat Syndrome Support Group Family Weekend is being held in Coventry, in May! [Register now](http://cdc2016.eventbrite.co.uk) to secure your place!

Registration is a fixed price per room, so if you need two rooms, please make sure you book two tickets, and enter the details of everyone who will be attending. Each room can accomodate up to 2 adults and 2 children under 12.

If you cannot afford to attend, or have any other questions, please [get in touch](/about/contact.html); we can help!
