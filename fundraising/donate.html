Donating to the Cri du Chat Syndrome Support Group is quick, easy, and helps us to support families in need.

You can make a one-off or regular donation through JustGiving. It's safe, easy, and we will automatically receive Gift Aid if you're a UK taxpayer.

If you'd prefer not to use JustGiving, you can also make an online donation through Paypal.

Other ways to donate

By Cheque

Please make cheques payable to "Cri du Chat Syndrome Support Group" and post to:

Ray Clarke, Treasurer, 5 Latimer Drive, Laindon, Essex, SS15 4AD

Payroll Giving

Make a regular contribution direct from your payslip. It's taken before tax, so a £10 donation would only cost you £8. Visit the gov.uk information page for more details.

Gift Aid

If you are a UK taxpayer, we can claim back the tax on your donations through Gift Aid. The scheme means that your gift can increase in value by nearly a third. Visit HMRC's information page for more details.

If you would like us to claim back the tax on your donations then please print out our Gift Aid declaration form, fill it in and send it to us.

Legacies

Leaving a gift in your will is simple and cost effective. You can leave a specific gift (such as stocks and shares, property, a coin collection etc), a monetary sum or a portion of your estate. Please help our work in the future and consider leaving a gift in your will. See the gov.uk charitable giving pages for more information.
